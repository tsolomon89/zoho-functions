# Renewal Call 4 — Value & Feature Pitch for Renewal

## Objective
Reiterate Jurnii’s value to the organization, highlight new features available upon renewal, and overcome churn risk.

## Call Script
"Hi `${Contacts.First_Name}`, `${User.First_Name}` from Jurnii. Hope you're doing well."

*(Wait for response)*

"I know how busy things are, so I'll be very brief. 

Over the past year, Jurnii has successfully resolved duplicate data and automated opportunities for `${Contacts.Account_Name}`. Upon renewal, we are exciting to unlock our new advanced matching module and custom product reporting features for your team at no extra cost. 

Let's schedule a brief 5-minute call to finalize our renewal options. What day works best?"

## Call Outcomes
- **Positive**: Renewal scheduled / agreed.
- **Neutral / No Answer**: Create next call. Send `Renewal Email 4`.
- **Negative**: Decided to churn.
