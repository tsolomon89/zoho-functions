# Marketing Qualification Call 4 — Qualification & Value Alignment

## Objective
Make a warm connection, address any hesitation, gather qualification data, and obtain consent.

## Call Script
"Hello `${Contacts.First_Name}`, this is `${User.First_Name}` calling from Jurnii. Hope you're doing well."

*(Wait for response)*

"I know things get incredibly busy, so I'll be very brief. We’re working with several organizations similar to `${Contacts.Account_Name}` to optimize their operations. I wanted to see if you were interested in receiving our latest case studies and product guides? If so, I just need your verbal consent to send them to your email."

*(If consent given)*
"Excellent. And to ensure the content is highly relevant to your department, are you primarily focused on operations, engineering, or strategy?"

## Call Outcomes
- **Positive**: Consent obtained, profile updated.
- **Neutral / No Answer**: Create next call.
- **Negative**: Mark DNC/Lost.
