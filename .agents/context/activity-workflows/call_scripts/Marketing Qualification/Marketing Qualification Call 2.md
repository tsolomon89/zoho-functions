# Marketing Qualification Call 2 — Follow-up & Details Gathering

## Objective
Follow up on initial contact to verify details, obtain Marketing Qualification, and complete profile fields.

## Prep Checklist
- Review Call 1 notes (if any).
- Note missing profile fields.

## Call Script

### Opening
"Hi `${Contacts.First_Name}`, `${User.First_Name}` from Jurnii here again. Hope your week is going well!"

*(Wait for response)*

"I’m reaching back out to briefly verify a couple of details on your profile at `${Contacts.Account_Name}` so we can tailor our insights for you. Do you have a quick moment?"

### Data Gathering
"We want to make sure you only receive information highly relevant to your role.
- What specific project or goal led you to check out Jurnii?
- And are you the main person overseeing this initiative, or are there others on your team involved?"

### Obtaining Consent
"Got it. To ensure you receive our specialized insights, benchmark studies, and product updates, can I get your consent to keep in touch via email?"

### Closing
"Super, thank you for your time. Have a great day!"

## Call Outcomes
- **Positive**: Consent obtained, profile updated.
- **Neutral / No Answer**: Create next call.
- **Negative**: Mark DNC/Lost.
