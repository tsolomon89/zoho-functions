# Commercials Sent Call 2 — Objection Handling & Contract Negotiation

## Objective
Handle pricing, contract length, or legal objections, and prompt discussion to keep the deal moving forward.

## Call Script

### Opening
"Hi `${Contacts.First_Name}`, `${User.First_Name}` from Jurnii. Hope you're having a good week!"

*(Wait for response)*

"I’m following up on our proposal for `${Contacts.Account_Name}`. I know your team is reviewing the details, and I wanted to see if there are any specific clauses, billing terms, or licensing structures that we should adjust to make this work for you?"

### Objection Handling Guide
- *Pricing too high*: "We can review tiered pricing structures or pilot scopes to fit your budget bounds. What is your target figure?"
- *Legal delay*: "We're happy to jump on a quick call with your legal counsel to clear up any standard clauses."

## Call Outcomes
- **Positive**: Terms accepted/Intent to sign.
- **Neutral / No Answer**: Create next call. Send `Commercials Sent Email 2`.
- **Negative**: Move to Lost.
