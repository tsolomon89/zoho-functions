# Renewal Call 2 — Expansion & Terms Discussion

## Objective
Present customized expansion or renewal terms, address licensing adjustments, and move the renewal to sign-off.

## Call Script

### Opening
"Hi `${Contacts.First_Name}`, `${User.First_Name}` from Jurnii. Hope your week is off to a great start!"

*(Wait for response)*

"Following up on our renewal review, I’m working with our commercial team to prepare `${Contacts.Account_Name}`’s renewal agreement. 

Based on your team's usage growth, we’ve included options for expanded seats and advanced duplicate mapping rules at a preferred rate. Do you have any feedback on those terms, or should we finalize the contract for signature?"

## Call Outcomes
- **Positive**: Renewal terms agreed / signed.
- **Neutral / No Answer**: Create next call. Send `Renewal Email 2`.
- **Negative**: Objection raised. Create retention review Task.
