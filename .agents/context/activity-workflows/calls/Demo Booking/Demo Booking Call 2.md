# Demo Booking Call 2 — Overcoming Hesitation & Presenting Value

## Objective
Address booking hesitation, reinforce demo value based on company context, and secure the booking.

## Call Script

### Opening
"Hi `${Contacts.First_Name}`, `${User.First_Name}` from Jurnii. Hope you're having a good week!"

*(Wait for response)*

"Following up on our email exchange, I wanted to quickly see if we could get that 15-minute tailored demo scheduled for `${Contacts.Account_Name}`. 

I know you are busy, which is exactly why this demo is valuable—we’ll show you how Jurnii automates up to 80% of manual data entry for teams like yours. What day works best for a quick run-through?"

### Overcoming Objections
- *No time*: "I completely understand. The walk-through is strictly 15 minutes, and it will save you hours of manual reporting every single week."
- *Send info*: "I've sent some details, but the real power of Jurnii is seeing how it integrates with your specific CRM fields. It's much faster to see it live."

## Call Outcomes
- **Positive**: Demo Confirmation.
- **Neutral / No Answer**: Create next call. Send `Demo Booking Email 2`.
- **Negative**: Mark Lost.
