# Marketing Consent Call 3 — Profile & Qualification Check-in

## Objective
Re-engage the prospect, resolve missing profile fields, and secure marketing consent.

## Call Script
"Hi `${Contacts.First_Name}`, `${User.First_Name}` from Jurnii. I hope you're having a good day."

*(Wait for response)*

"I wanted to quickly check in on whether you've had a chance to review the resources we sent over to `${Contacts.Account_Name}`. While I have you, we're currently finalizing our industry-specific benchmark reports. To make sure we send you the correct edition, what is your current primary focus or role?"

*(Gather title, role, and details)*

"And to ensure we have permission to email this report and other tailormade product updates directly to you, do you give us consent to stay in touch via email?"

## Call Outcomes
- **Positive**: Consent obtained, profile updated.
- **Neutral / No Answer**: Create next call.
- **Negative**: Mark DNC/Lost.
